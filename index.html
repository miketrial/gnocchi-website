The Watch Flag — your entry-trigger alarm
Mark a stock now, get a green flash the moment it becomes actionable.
1Spot a setup — look for Trend, 3M Mom, and Near Hi all green in the table.
2Click the flag next to the ticker — it turns yellow (Watching). Saved locally; survives refresh.
3Wait for auto-promote — screener checks every render. When score ≥ 22 and any of T/V/P is green, it flips green (Ready) and the row flashes.
4Check the 3 dots on the Ready chip — all green means the entry timing is right. Wait for 3/3. Yellow = signal was green within the last 24h, rescan to confirm.
Ready requires: Score ≥ 22 · at least one of T/V/P green (or green within last 24h)
Demoted = score fell below 22 or all 3 signals lost for 24h. × dismisses the alert.
Treat the green flash as a "check this now" signal, not an autopilot buy.
