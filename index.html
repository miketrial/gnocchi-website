Bounce scoring  ·  bidirectional BUY/SELL, tuned for a 1-2 day mean-reversion hold
This is a genuinely different trade philosophy from Swing — that view is trend-following (buy strength, ride weeks); this one bets on a snap-back after price gets stretched, in either direction (oversold dip = BUY, overbought top = SELL). Lower hit-rate by design, so earnings is a hard gate, and Liquidity/ADR dampen both Buy and Sell scores rather than just docking points on one side.

The Watch Flag — your entry-trigger alarm
Mark a stock now, get a green flash the moment it becomes actionable.
1Spot a setup — look for Trend, 3M Mom, and Near Hi all green in the table.
2Click the flag next to the ticker — it turns yellow (Watching). Saved locally; survives refresh.
3Wait for auto-promote — screener checks every render. When score ≥ 22 and any of T/V/P is green, it flips green (Ready) and the row flashes.
4Check the 3 dots on the Ready chip — all green means the entry timing is right. Wait for 3/3. Yellow = signal was green within the last 24h, rescan to confirm.
Ready requires: Score ≥ 22 · at least one of T/V/P green (or green within last 24h)
Demoted = score fell below 22 or all 3 signals lost for 24h. × dismisses the alert.
Treat the green flash as a "check this now" signal, not an autopilot buy.

What the numbers mean
Win rate
Share of closed trades that made money.
Avg P/L
Average % gain/loss per closed trade.
Median hold
Typical days a trade stayed open.
Best / Worst
Biggest single winner and loser.
Cumulative
Total $ if you'd put a fixed $10,000 into every trade.
Edge vs SPY
Your avg P/L minus what SPY returned over the same trade dates — did timing the bounce beat just being in the market? Positive = the signal added value.
Open
A BUY still running. Now is the live price, Unrealized the paper gain so far.
Closed
A finished BUY → SELL trade. Exit on is the verdict that closed it (always SELL under the current rule).
The 15-day seed is a historical replay of the EOD factors (after-hours and VIX legs can't be reconstructed, so those days may differ slightly from what the live tool showed). Forward datapoints are recorded each time you open the app or rescan, at the price that scan saw. No slippage or commissions. Cumulative $ assumes a fixed $10,000 per trade.
